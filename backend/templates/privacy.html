Privacy Policy for Watched_

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The "Watched_" Chrome Extension collects the following information to function:

  • Authentication Data: We access your authentication cookies to verify your identity with the Watched_ service.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on which movies/shows you mark as "Watched" to sync with your dashboard.

2. How We Use Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Display your watch history status overlaid on Google Search results.
  • Sync your "Watched" status modifications to your personal dashboard.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties.

4. Contact

For questions, please contact the developer.
